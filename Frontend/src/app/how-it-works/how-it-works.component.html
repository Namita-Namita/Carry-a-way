How it works

Ship your parcels with us without worrying about the expense and safe delivery.

We can all agree that dragging shipments around is awful in almost every way. If you’re wondering how to return or pickup parcels, Carry-A-Way has made it easy and affordable. We let you ship on your terms by giving you options, and we also make sure you pay nowhere near what everyone else pays. Here’s how it works:

1. Get labels

Bags aren’t very smart, so each one will need a shipping label attached in order to get it to its destination. You can get your labels in three ways: 1) print them out the old fashioned way, 2) opt to have Digital Labels sent to your phone, or 3) have us mail you pre-printed labels. You must securely attach it to your bag and place a copy of the label inside of your bag just in case. Your shipments is going to be traveling far and wide, and your label is your ticket! If your label is not properly attached, it is at risk of falling off in transit. Without its ticket, your bag will probably be delayed, and may even be lost.

2. Send shipments

Now you do not need to get your shipment to UPS or FedEx. You can Fpay for a pickup at your home, work, or hotel during booking. You can drop your shipment off early or late, but that will probably affect the delivery date. Learn more about your options for sending shipment.

3. Travel light

This is the “travel without the shipment” part. Grab your iPad and head to the airport. Track your Carry-A-Way shipment from anywhere. We’ll notify you when your shipment arrives. If you do have any questions regarding your shipment status, it is more efficient to contact our carriers directly. They have 24/7 support for any shipping related questions!

4. Arrive relaxed

UPS or FedEx will deliver the shipment directly to your destination address and you get to skip the dreaded baggage claim. If you will not be available to accept your shipment, check out these other parcel delivery options. Please be sure to notify our carriers if you have any special delivery instructions such as security codes. If our carriers are unable to deliver due to an incorrect or incomplete address, your delivery date will be delayed.
